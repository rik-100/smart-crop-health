        About

        Built for Farmers,
        Powered by AI

        CropSense AI is an intelligent plant disease detection system designed to help farmers and agronomists identify crop diseases early, understand severity, and take prompt action to protect their harvest.

        Using a fine-tuned MobileNetV2 architecture trained on the PlantVillage dataset, the model achieves high accuracy across 15 common disease classes. The Grad-CAM visualization adds explainability, making the AI transparent and trustworthy.
